Diplomatic Gathering

Now that you have familiarized yourself with the aliens on Ginuli, it’s time to make arrangements for your first event.

You will be hosting a diplomatic gathering and selecting different delegations of aliens to invite.

It is very important that this gathering be successful.

One way to do this involves paying attention to the aethetics of the aliens’ outfits.

Aesthetics matter a lot in making a good impression. We can try to control this by paying attention to clothing and who’s wearing what.

As you will have noticed, some aliens on Ginuli wear blue outfits and some wear black outfits,

and it would be a shame if the colors weren’t harmonized at the gathering.

We should therefore try to have an equal number of each color outfit at the gathering.

On each of the next few screens you will be presented with two delegations of aliens (often of different sizes) and you must pick one in each case.

In doing so, please try to keep an equal number of blue and black outfits overall.

If the colors end up unbalanced, we will not make the impact we hope for!
